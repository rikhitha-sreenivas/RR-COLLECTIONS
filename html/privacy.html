Data Collection

R&R COLLECTIONS collects personal identifiers such as names and email addresses only when provided through our contact or garage forms. We use browser LocalStorage to save your vehicle shortlists, ensuring your data remains on your device.

Information Usage

The information collected is used strictly for automotive acquisition inquiries and fleet management within the showroom. We prioritize the security of our collectors and do not share data with third parties.
