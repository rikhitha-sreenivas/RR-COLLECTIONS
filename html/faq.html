QUESTIONS

01. FLEET & ACQUISITION

What is the "Garage" feature? +
The Garage allows you to shortlist your favorite bikes and cars. It saves your selection in your browser so you can review your fleet later without needing to login.
How do I finalize a purchase? +
Once you've shortlisted your vehicles, use the Contact Us page to send your inquiry. Our Vijayawada concierge team will then reach out to finalize documentation and road tax.
Are these limited-edition models? +
Yes, many machines in our showroom, including the Apsara series, are part of limited production runs available exclusively through R&R COLLECTIONS.

02. LOGISTICS & SUPPORT

What are the delivery timelines? +
Standard delivery for ex-showroom models is 7-10 business days. Custom configurations may take longer depending on technical specifications.
Is the pricing inclusive of GST? +
No, all listed prices are ex-showroom. Final on-road pricing includes the mandatory 18% GST, insurance, and RTO registration in Andhra Pradesh.
Can I book a test ride? +
Absolutely. Appointments for the 650cc fleet and our premium car lineup can be scheduled via the contact form or directly at our Vijayawada center.
